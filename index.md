# Privacy Policy for Naija News Flash✅

## Introduction
Naija News Flash✅ is an Alexa Flash Briefing skill that delivers Nigerian news headlines directly to your Alexa-enabled device. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Information Collection
This skill does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information from users.  
We do not request names, email addresses, or any other identifying details.

## Use of Information
Since no personal information is collected, none is used, stored, or shared.  
The skill only provides headline summaries of Nigerian news.

## Third-Party Services
Naija News Flash✅ does not integrate with or share data with third-party services.  
All content is delivered through Alexa’s Flash Briefing framework.

## Children’s Privacy
This skill is safe for all audiences. It does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
